About Us

SunTrack is Sunway University's official lost and found platform, designed to help students and staff recover misplaced items efficiently and securely. Recognizing the limitations of informal platforms, SunTrack offers a centralized, user-friendly, and mobile-responsive website where users can report, search, and reconnect with lost belongings. Our mission is to simplify the recovery process, foster a connected campus community, and provide a reliable space for lost and found services through intuitive design and seamless user interaction.
