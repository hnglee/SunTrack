Recently Lost

1.How do I report a lost item?

You can report a lost item by using the upload function in the 'Lost Items' page.

2. How do I report a found item?

You can report a found item by using the upload function in the 'Found Items' page.

3. How do I manage my account settings?

You can manage your account settings by clicking on your profile picture in the upper right corner.

4. How can I contact the finder of my lost item or the owner of a found item?

If you find an item displayed on the page that you or someone else has lost, you can expand the item and send a Message. The button will appear. You can use this button to contact the person who found the item or the owner.

5. What should I do if I forgot my password?

If you forget your password and cannot log in, you can click 'Forgot Password ?' to reset your password.
